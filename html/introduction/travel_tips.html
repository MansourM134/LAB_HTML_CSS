Travel tips for Saudi Arabia

Respect Local Culture

Saudi Arabia has strong cultural and Islamic traditions. Visitors should dress modestly and behave respectfully in public places.

Dress Code

It is recommended to wear clothing that covers shoulders and knees. Loose and comfortable clothes are best for the climate

Weather & Climate

Saudi Arabia has a hot desert climate, with summer temperatures reaching over 40°C. Stay hydrated and avoid the sun during midday.

Money & Payments

The currency is the Saudi Riyal (SAR). Credit cards are widely accepted, but carrying some cash is useful for small purchases.

Language

Arabic is the official language, but English is commonly spoken in cities, making it easy for tourists to communicate.

Transportation

Saudi Arabia has modern infrastructure. You can travel using cars, ride apps, trains, or domestic flights between cities.

Laws & Rules

Alcohol is prohibited, and public behavior should remain respectful. Always follow local laws and regulations.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit is during winter (November to March), when temperatures are cooler and more comfortable.
